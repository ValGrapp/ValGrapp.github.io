My Toolset

Deeper insights in my design prozess

The design thinking process is a great approach for a elaborated project. I want to use this page to give you deeper insights into my proceedings and show you a selection of the methods which often support me and my teams to get to our goal. Across all those steps it is important to say that it is never a straight prozess. We sometimes have to jump back and forth and often iterate individual steps. And in general not every methods fits to every projects. It is also our task to evaluate which ones will be the most productive ones for this specific case.

    Overview

01 Empathy

Day in a life | Be your own customer | Culture Probe

As a first step I want to know who I am designing for and realize and feel the needs of my users.

02 Synthesis

❮
❯

User Journey | Mind Map | HMW | Empathy Map | Storyboard | Persona

After a period of research I often feel overwhelmed by the amount of insights I did collect. The above stated methods are an immense help to identify the most important aspects and to sharpen your perspective.

03 Ideation

Crazy 8 | Brainstorming

On this point I we already know which problem we want to solve. But how will we do it? It is time to widen our minds again and to allow every crazy thought that comes in our mind. Nothing is too unrealistic or too insane. The base of the final idea or solution lays in all of them.

04 Prototyp

Paper Prototyp to Wireframes Clickdummy | Arduino | Tinker

After defining the solution, it is finally time to get not just visual but also to make the project come alive. Which this methods we form a perfect foundation for following user tests.

05 Test

Think aloud | Shadowing

Now it is time to find out which aspects of our prototyp or concept in general pass the inspection of our users. It is always important to clarify the users about the fact that we are testing our product, not them and that the can not do anything wrong. If something does not work out, it is a weakness of the product. With this precious insights we can interate our product until it is finally well-engineered.
