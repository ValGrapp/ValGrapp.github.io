Hey there!


I am an enthusiastic designer who aims to detect human pain points/needs through empathy and curiosity and solve them with creativity. On that way, I am always seeking to learn something new and collaborate with people from all different skill sets to create interdisciplinary and complex solutions. The combination of design and code offers me to think through and understand the overall creation prozess of a new product. On the theoretical side I love to inform myself about tech related news and discuss about technical sociology and sustainability. My desire to be a designer profounds in my dream to contribute something impactful to the world. And I really love to tinker, prototyping is quite close to that.
